muuto | outline chair taupe leg | twill 280

Brand: MUUTO NEW NORDIC

new
Dimensions: 88 x 84 x 69.5cm high | 40cm seat height
EXCLUSIVE TO TOP3 | Limited stock arriving. If no arrival date shown, they have been sold and all Exclusive Twill fabric Outline sofas are made to order with a 12-14 week lead time.

Outline series is available in different various fabric or leather options. Price shown is the starting price in fabric. Please contact us for fabric and leather price variations.

Visually light and elegant sofa series with deep seating for high comfort. 

From the designers: “With the Outline series, we aimed to create a visually light and elegant sofa series with lots of comfort. We wanted to translate some of the elemental and poetic qualities we find inspiring in modernistic architecture. The way some elements juxtapose in the design are borrowed from the way houses are structured.
The different planes overlap and give lightness to the expression. This is why Outline has a certain calm serenity - it offers great comfort in a generous seat, but has a slender appearance, and a strong architectural presence.” - says Anderssen Voll about the design

Material:
Powder coated aluminum legs. Wood and steel construction. Cold foam granulate and feather filling.

Cleaning instructions:

For fabric: Remove non-greasy stains by carefully dabbing with a lint-free cloth wrung out in clean, warm water. If necessary, clean by dabbing with soapy water. Visit Kvadrat.com for further information on cleaning textiles.

For leather: please find cleaning instructions at ca-mo.com.

EXCLUSIVE TO TOP3 BY DESIGN

Muuto's simple and elegant Outline Sofa Series in a new exclusive version, upholstered in the Twill Weave from Kvadrat for a warm and sophisticated expression—available for a limited time only.

Exclusive version is available in 4 different colours.
